This is a decision-support and early-warning tool, not a validated capital or provisioning model. Forecasts are derived from a HAC-corrected regression on Malaysian industrial output (DOSM), oil price (EIA), and global supply chain stress (NY Fed GSCPI), tested for statistical robustness — sectors flagged "not significant" should not be treated as reliable predictions.
